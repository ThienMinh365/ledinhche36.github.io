ABOUT US

In 2012 James Bruce from the UK, and Toshihiro Yoshida PhD. from Japan, founded RedFin Aquarium Design. James and Toshi aimed to go beyond building ordinary fish tanks and instead create one of a kind aquatic masterpieces for customers across the globe.
Since its inception, RedFin has grown to a dedicated team of designers, mechanical and structural engineers, aquarists and biologists based throughout three offices worldwide.
RedFin's Team have worked on projects ranging from luxury, custom residential aquariums right up to some of the largest public aquariums in the world.
RedFin is now considered one of the world's leading aquarium design and construction specialists.
